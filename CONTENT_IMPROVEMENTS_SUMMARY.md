## Content Quality Improvements

### Writing Style
- ✅ UK spelling used throughout ("travelling", "favourite")
- ✅ Short sentences (15-20 words average)
- ✅ Active voice where possible
- ✅ Plain English, no jargon
- ✅ Respectful tone for Islamic context
- ✅ Direct address using "you" and "your"

### Keyword Placement
- ✅ Primary keywords in H1s
- ✅ Keywords in first 100-150 words
- ✅ Secondary keywords in H2s naturally
- ✅ Long-tail keywords in body content
- ✅ No keyword stuffing

### CTAs
- ✅ At least one CTA above the fold on key pages
- ✅ CTAs near the end of long content
- ✅ Specific action verbs: "View", "Buy", "Get", "Check"
- ✅ Clear, specific CTAs: "View Umrah eSIM Plans" not "Click Here"

### Trust Signals
- ✅ Money-back guarantee mentioned
- ✅ 24/7 support availability highlighted
- ✅ Activation guarantee emphasised
- ✅ Coverage guarantee stated
- ✅ Social proof (10,000+ customers, 4.8/5 rating)
