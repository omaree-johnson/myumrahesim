## Brand Voice & Tone

- **Clear and reassuring:** Simple explanations, no jargon
- **Respectful:** Appropriate tone for Islamic pilgrimage context
- **Practical:** Focus on solving real problems for pilgrims
- **Plain English:** Short sentences, UK spelling where relevant
- **Trust-building:** Emphasise reliability, support, and guarantees

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Muslims travelling for Umrah and Hajj
- **Geographic:** UK, Europe, US, Gulf countries
- **Tech level:** Mixed - many are not tech-savvy, need simple guidance
- **Pain points:**
  - Worried about staying connected during pilgrimage
  - Need to contact family back home
  - Require navigation and access to Nusuk app
  - Want to avoid expensive roaming charges
  - Prefer simple, no-hassle solutions

## Content Guidelines

### Writing Style
- Use UK spelling: "travelling", "favourite", "colour"
- Short sentences (15-20 words average)
- Active voice where possible
- Avoid jargon; explain technical terms
- Use "you" and "your" to address pilgrims directly

### Keyword Placement
- Primary keyword in H1
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Secondary keywords in H2s naturally
- Long-tail keywords in body content
- Never keyword stuff

### CTAs
- At least one CTA above the fold
- One CTA near the end of long content
- Use action verbs: "View", "Buy", "Get", "Check"
- Make CTAs specific: "View Umrah eSIM Plans" not just "Click Here"

### Trust Signals
- Money-back guarantee
- 24/7 support availability
- Activation guarantee
- Coverage guarantee
- Customer testimonials
- Social proof (number of customers)
