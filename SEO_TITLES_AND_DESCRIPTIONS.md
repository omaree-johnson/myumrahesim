## Title & Description Guidelines

### Title Best Practices
- Keep under 60 characters
- Place primary keyword near the front
- Include brand or location when relevant
- Use pipe (|) to separate main title from qualifier
- Include price when appropriate (for plans page)

### Meta Description Best Practices
- Keep under 155-160 characters
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Mention key benefits (instant activation, coverage)
- Include price when relevant
- End with a benefit or CTA
- Make it compelling to click

### H1 Best Practices
- One H1 per page
- Include primary keyword
- Clear and descriptive
- Match user intent
- 50-70 characters ideal
